In order to get this hal running, you will need to place the following files 
from the developer/evaluation kit in the hal/drivers subdirectory

Important:
The header-files (*.h) have to be placed in the appropriate subdirectories of
hal/drivers while the implementation files (*.c) must reside directly in the hal/drivers
directory.


The files can be found at the following locations in the kit:

startup_gcc.c 
driverlib/sysctl.c 
driverlib/interrupt.c 
driverlib/cpu.c
driverlib/uart.c 
driverlib/gpio.c 
driverlib/i2c.c 
drivers/osram96x16x1.c 
driverlib/watchdog.c 
driverlib/systick.c
